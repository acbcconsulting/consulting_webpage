MVP 2.0 Equity Partner

ACBC Consulting is an official Equity Partner of the Massachusetts Executive Office of Energy and Environmental Affairs (EEA) Municipal Vulnerability Preparedness (MVP) 2.0 program.

As an MVP Equity Partner, we support municipalities across the Commonwealth in centering equity in their climate resilience planning — surfacing the invisible structures that determine who is protected, who carries risk, and who shapes the response.
